Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a heavenly dish, straight from the homelands of Cicero into your mouth.

Lasagna contains:

  • Sheets of pasta
  • Canned, crushed tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • ground beef
  • garlic
  • onions
  • bechamel sauce

    Steps:

    1. Fry all the stuff
    2. Put in cacerole with pasta and bechamel sauce
    3. Oven
